How It Works

Four Simple Steps to Peace of Mind

1
📞

Schedule a Visit

Call us or book online. Share the appointment details and any special needs — wheelchair, oxygen, or just a comforting hand to hold.

2
🚗

We Pick Them Up

A verified companion arrives at the door — on time, every time. They help your parent into a clean, comfortable vehicle.

Our companion stays with them throughout — registration, waiting, consultation, pharmacy, and tests. You get real-time updates.
